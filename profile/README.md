## Essay Guidelines

In this class you will be asked to write a number of essays.
All of these essays **must be written by you**, I want to know what you think.
Assignments marked with "Essay" will use the following requirements.

## Format

All essays in this class are to be in [APA](https://apastyle.apa.org/instructional-aids/beginner-student-paper-checklist.pdf) format.
Follow these specific requirements:

- Font: Use a standard, readable font like Times New Roman, 12-point.
- Line Spacing: The entire document must be double-spaced.
- Header: The header on each page should include your last name and the page number in the top right corner.
- Citations: You must properly cite all sources, both in-text and on a separate References page, using APA guidelines.
    - You **can** cite AI. Just know this should not be a primary source. You need to cite AI as
      well [like so](https://apastyle.apa.org/blog/how-to-cite-chatgpt).
- Length: Unless otherwise specified, all essays should be between 300 and 600 words.

## Accuracy

A lot of the papers in this class will be option based / persuasive.
I want to hear your opinion **BUT** your claims must be instanced.
Meaning if you say "AI hurts the environment because of its large energy usage." you need cite a source for that claim.
The source of this claim must be reliable.
**AI is not a reliable source.**

## Recommended Work flow

I will give some advice to writing your paper.
Spend the time planning its always worth it.
Anything with  emoji means you **can** use AI to help.
Don't let AI slop up your paper.

1. Open your word processor and copy the prompt.
2.  Do as much research into the topic as hand. Writing down links to the resources.
3.  Come up with the main thesis statement or argument you want to make.
4. Create a rough outline of your essay. I like a bullet list.
5. Write the first draft, focusing only on getting your ideas down.
6. Take a break. This really does help.
7. Revise your draft for clarity and content. Check that your arguments are strong and your claims are supported by
   sources.
8.  Edit the paper for grammar, spelling, and punctuation.
    - **DO NOT USE AI TO "PARAPHRASE" WRITING**.
9.  Finally, format the entire document according to the guidelines listed above.
